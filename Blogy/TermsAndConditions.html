Terms and conditions of Blogy

Your content

Blogy is a free-opened social network.
That means your story will be public and everybody will be able to read and see it.

But does everything will be public ?
Of course no.
In Blogy we organized the content and we decide to make some sections private.
These sections are :
1. Your Album
2. Your messages
3. Your friends
What can you share in your story ?
Here in Blogy we think that everyone have to choose what to share. So we let you to choose what is good enough for your story.
But we don't allow you to post those kind of stories :
1. Stories that harass someone or something privacy
2. Brutal content of corpses and dead bodies
3. Murderers and criminal stories
If you found something you don't like, just report it to us :)
How we suggest you friends ?
We get your friends, check their friends and we filter them after that we suggest you the best matches for your.

Friends

In Blogy we believe that when you follow someone you will want him as friend.
That is two sides process which means, that other bloger will add you to their friends list in the same way just like you.

What is the Ohana ?
The members in your Ohana must be unique.
They will stay in your Ohana even if you block them. Ohana members are just like your "Close friends" in Facebook. So choose your Ohana members carefully.
