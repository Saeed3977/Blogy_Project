Your content

Blogy is a free-opened social network.
That means your story will be public and everybody will be able to read and see it.

But does everything will be public ?
Of course no.
In Blogy we organized the content and we decided to make some sections private.
These sections are :
1. Your Album
2. Your messages
3. Your friends
What can you share in your story ?
Here in Blogy we think that everyone can choose what to share. So we let you to choose what is good enough for your story.
But we don't allow you to post those kind of stories :
1. Stories that harass someone or someones privacy
2. Brutal and violent content
3. Pornographic materials
If you find something inappropriate, report it and we will review it.
How we suggest you friends ?
We get your friends, check their friends and we filter them after that we suggest you the best matches for your.

Friends

In Blogy we believe that when you follow someone you will want him as a friend.

What is the "Ohana" ?
The members in your Ohana must be unique.
They will stay in your Ohana even if you block them. Ohana members are just like your "Close friends" in Facebook. So choose your Ohana members carefully.
