What is Blogy ?

Blogy is the place where you can share your own story. It is a place where everyone is connected and everyone has the possibility to share everything that they like.

How it works ?

Well actually it is pretty simple.
Just register and start blogy-ing.

What is the difference ?

Blogy is for the bloggers, for the people with stories.
For those who want to share their #unique and #personal stories with others.
You have to try and feel the experience that makes the difference between Blogy and others.
